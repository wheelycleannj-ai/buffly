Everything in one place

Built around how a detail shop actually runs

No bloated generic CRM. Every screen is made for booking cars, routing vans, and getting paid.

Online booking that converts

A branded, mobile-first booking page. Customers pick a service, vehicle size, add-ons, and time in under two minutes — and pay a deposit to lock it in.

Smart scheduling & van routes

Day, week, and map views with per-van lanes. One tap builds the shortest route for the day and hands your crew turn-by-turn directions for every stop.

Quotes, invoices & deposits

Turn a quote into an invoice in a click, collect deposits up front, and always know what's still owed across every job.

Gift cards & coupon codes

Sell gift cards right on your booking page and run promo codes customers redeem at checkout. Balances and usage track themselves.

Reviews on autopilot & win-back

Automatically ask for a Google review the moment a job is done, and resurface customers who've gone quiet with a one-tap offer.

Analytics that matter

A monthly P&L, your top services, where your money actually comes from, and repeat-customer rate — so you spend on what works.

Job checklists & SOPs

Per-service checklists mean every car — by every tech — gets the same five-star process. Great for training new crew fast.

Your brand, not ours

Fully white-label: your logo, your colors, light or dark theme — on both the staff console and the customer booking page.

Customer import in minutes

Bring your existing customer list over with a CSV — it maps names, phones, vehicles and addresses for you, so you start day one with your book of business.
